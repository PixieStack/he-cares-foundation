The He Cares Foundation

He Cares Foundation is a transformative movement dedicated to inspiring and equipping young boys and men in South Africa to become protectors, nurturers, and beacons of hope in their communities. Through faith, mentorship, and real-world action, we are raising a generation of men who lead with empathy, courage, and responsibility.

Our vision extends beyond individual transformation—we strive for a culture shift across South Africa, where men are celebrated for their compassion and their contributions to a safer, more just society. The He Cares Foundation partners with families, schools, and local organizations to create sustainable pathways for growth, healing, and opportunity.

By combining mentorship, education, and advocacy, we empower young men to challenge stereotypes and to live out values of respect and dignity. Together, we are building a movement that supports, uplifts, and protects everyone in our communities.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To nurture a new generation of South African men who lead with empathy, courage, and a deep sense of responsibility for the safety and upliftment of women and children. Through mentorship, workshops, outreach programs, and faith-based guidance, we turn this vision into reality—one young life at a time.

Vision: We envision communities where boys are taught that real power is shown in compassion, and where men are seen as protectors, mentors, and allies in the fight for social justice. Our goal is a nation where every man stands as a beacon of hope and healing.

A Voice for Change

Godfrey uses his public platform to advocate for real, lasting change. Whether through powerful monologues, interviews, or outreach events, his message is consistent: "Real men uplift, protect, and serve." He is regularly invited to radio shows, community events, and schools to speak about positive masculinity.

More Than a Movement

For Godfrey, He Cares is a calling. His leadership is defined by humility, courage, and relentless hope. Every project, every partnership, and every song is a testament to his belief in a brighter, more compassionate future for all. He is committed to walking with the youth, not just speaking to them.

On Stage & Screen

With a career spanning drama, reality TV, and music, Godfrey brings authenticity and presence to every role. His work has resonated with viewers across South Africa, making him a familiar face and a storyteller with heart. He believes in the power of media to create positive change and uses every opportunity to advocate for compassion and justice.

In the Community

Driven by faith and a passion for justice, Godfrey leads workshops, mentors youth, and speaks at schools—empowering boys and men to show up for their sisters, mothers, and neighbours. His vision: a nation where men are healers, not harmers. He's also actively involved in grassroots projects, supporting families and youth initiatives.

"Changing the world starts with how we treat the women and children closest to us. My journey is about inspiring boys and men to lead with love, courage, and faith—on screen, on stage, and in our streets."
