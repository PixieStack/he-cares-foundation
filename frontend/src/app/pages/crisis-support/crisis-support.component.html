Crisis Support & Referrals

At He Cares Foundation, we connect women and children in crisis with compassionate professionals and essential services. Whether you need urgent help, legal guidance, safe accommodation, or support for your children, our team is here for you.

All our services are confidential, free, and tailored to your needs.

If you are in immediate danger, call our 24/7 crisis line: 068 126 3127

What We Offer

  • Social Worker Support Confidential guidance, trauma support, and ongoing care for women and children in crisis.
  • Legal Referrals Connections to trusted lawyers for urgent legal assistance and advice, including protection orders and custody matters.
  • Child Support Play therapy, school referrals, and access to child protection services.
  • Emergency Shelter Safe accommodation and assistance with transport to secure locations.

How It Works

  • Submit a Request Tell us your situation and the support you need. Our secure form is quick and easy to complete.
  • Upload Documents If needed, upload referral letters or supporting documents to help us assist you faster.
  • Quick Response Our team will contact you within 48 hours with the next steps and immediate support options.
  • Continuous Support We stay connected and advocate for you, offering ongoing care and follow-up throughout your journey.

Who Can Refer?

  • Individuals (self-referral) You can reach out directly if you need help for yourself or your children.
  • Professionals & Advocates Social workers, legal professionals, or community advocates can refer on behalf of someone in need.
  • Family or Friends Loved ones can refer if the person in need has given their consent.
  • Our Focus For women and children facing crisis, abuse, or in need of urgent support.
